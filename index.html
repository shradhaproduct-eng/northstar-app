A few honest answers
Questions people ask
before starting.
Do my circle members need to download the app?
No. They only need WhatsApp. They respond in their normal chat and their messages appear in your app. Zero friction for them.
What if I miss a week? Or three?
Nothing. No guilt. No broken streaks. When you come back, North Star simply says — "Welcome back. Let's go." That's it.
Is this just another notification app?
No. North Star sends you one thing — a message from someone who cares about you. That's not a notification. That's a reason to open the app.
Can I keep my goal completely private?
Yes. No circles. No sharing. Just you, your North Star, your vision, and your plan. Private by default. Your Corner is always your choice.
